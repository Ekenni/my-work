About Me

I graduated from Iowa Western in 2024 with my A.A.S. in Web Design and Development and A.A.S in Web Applications and Computer Programing. I was interning at Sandhills Global while in school and was offered a full-time Interactive Design position upon graduating.

I have since been designing and building websites, conducting SEO audits for existing sites, and consulting with dealers for their website needs. Within my first year full-time I designed and built over 50 new websites. I have also started training new employees for the company.

I love getting to use my creative side to design custom user-friendly websites, but I find the most joy out of building websites and making the designs come to life.
